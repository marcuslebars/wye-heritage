**Selected Approach: Idea 2 — Dark Marine Premium**

This approach best reflects A1 Marine Care's existing brand identity (black + cyan logo) and positions Brandy's Cove Marina as a premium service destination. The dark background makes the cyan accents pop dramatically, and the full-width layout gives pricing tables room to breathe.
